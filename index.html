1. Computer-Designed Organisms:


By combining AI design methods with a cell-based construction toolkit,
a scalable pipeline for designing and creating novel organisms is introduced.

Kriegman, S.*, Blackiston, D.*, Levin, M., Bongard, J. (2020)
"A scalable pipeline for designing reconfigurable organisms",
Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS), vol. 117 no. 4, pp. 1853-1859.
Images, videos, code, and press coverage can be found here.

2. Living Robot Swarms:

Blackiston, D.*, Lederer E.*, Kriegman, S., Garnier, S., Bongard, J., Levin, M. (2021)
"A Cellular Platform for the Development of Synthetic Living Machines",
Science Robotics, vol. 6 no. 52, pp. eabf1571.
